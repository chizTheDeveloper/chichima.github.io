The Problem

Online shopping is fragmented. Consumers waste time and money switching between countless apps and websites to compare prices and find the best deals. There's a disconnect between physical and digital shopping, making it hard to make confident purchasing decisions.

üîÑ

Fragmented Experience

Users jump between multiple apps and websites, losing time and context in the process.

üí∞

Price Opacity

Lack of transparency makes it difficult to find the best deals and compare prices effectively.

‚ùì

Purchase Uncertainty

Disconnect between digital and physical worlds creates doubt about product fit and quality.

The Solution

Prism is a mobile-first platform that centralizes the shopping experience. It uses AI to aggregate prices in real-time and AR to bridge the gap between the digital and physical worlds, helping users shop smarter, not harder.

Key Innovations

AI

Intelligent Aggregation

Real-time price comparison across multiple retailers with AI-powered recommendations.

AR

Augmented Reality

Scan real-world products to instantly find online deals and alternatives.

UX

Unified Experience

Single platform that eliminates the need to switch between multiple shopping apps.
