The Problem

For many health-conscious individuals, especially busy professionals, managing energy levels is a constant struggle. The infamous "3 PM slump" is a common complaint.

Existing nutrition apps often exacerbate the problem by focusing on tedious calorie tracking and restrictive dieting, which can be unsustainable and ignore the bio-individuality of metabolism. Users lack a tool that can answer a simple question: "What should I eat, and when, to feel my best?"

The 'Daily Rhythm Score'

To move away from calorie-centric thinking, we introduced a single, gamified metric. The Rhythm Score (0-100) is calculated based on factors that promote metabolic health, such as meal timing consistency and positive energy correlations.

Design Solution

This score is displayed prominently on the dashboard in a vibrant, easy-to-read card. It gives users a clear, positive target to aim for each day that isn't tied to weight or restriction.

The Weekly Insights Report

At the end of each week, the app synthesizes the user's data into a beautiful, easy-to-digest report. It avoids overwhelming users with raw data, instead telling a story.

Design Solution

The report uses natural language headlines like "Your Top Energy Booster was..." and "Your #1 Energy Drainer was...". Simple bar charts visualize the findings, and the page culminates in a single, clear, actionable goal for the week ahead.
